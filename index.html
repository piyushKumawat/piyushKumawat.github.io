Hello!

I am a final year master's student at the Department of Chemical and Biochemical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology, Patna. Here, I work under the supervision of Dr. Nitin Dutt Chaturvedi in Process Systems Engineering Laboratory. At the same time, I am employed as Senior Research Fellow (SRF) in the R&D department of the institute under a project sponsored by SERB, India. My research interests are to develop data-driven methodologies for process industries, handling uncertainties in process engineering applications, optimization and scheduling. My current research comprises such mathematical modelling applications in the field of Process Systems Engineering.

Previously, as an undergraduate researcher, I worked over separation processes and mass transfer applications. I have a Bachelor of Engineering degree from Thapar Institue of Engineering and Technology, Patiala. There I worked under the supervision of Dr. Jai Prakash Kushwaha and Dr. Neetu Singh. I have also done industrial training at Essar Oil, Jamnagar (second largest refinery in India) and Shree Cement, Rajasthan. For more details, refer to my CV or drop me an email!
